====== Cloud Service Types ======
See [[Knowledgebase:Networking:Troubleshooting|Network Troubleshooting]]
X as a service
Infrastructure as a Service IaaS - rented hardware, no CapEx, low skill needed, highly agile and flexible
Platform as a service PaaS - no CapEx, users don't need to configure servers, productivity-focused, however may have platform limitations
Software as a service SaaS - Centrally hosted, pay as you go, limited by software provider
