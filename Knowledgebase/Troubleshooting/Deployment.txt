====== Deployment ======
Imaging - format a machine with an image of another machine

Disk cloning tools - makes a copy of an entire disk for backup, allows you to copy settings and folders, Clonezilla
	disk-to-disk - connect external hard drive to computer you want to clone

In linux, you can use __dd__ to clone folders and discs.
https://linuxhint.com/dd_command_linux/

OS vendors also often offer network deployments.
