RJ11 - 6 position, 2 conductor (6P2C) telephone connection. RJ14 has 4 conductors, often used for dual lines

BNC - bayonet neill-concelman, coax connector, rigid and bulky, twists and locks

F-connector - cable tv and modem, RG-6 or RG-59 cable, threaded connector must be twisted

USB 1-3.2, A, B, C

Lightning - apple, 8pin
