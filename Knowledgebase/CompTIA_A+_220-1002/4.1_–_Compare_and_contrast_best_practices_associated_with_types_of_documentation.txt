Organizations have different policies and procedures.

Knowledge base
	external - manufacturer kb, internet communities
	Internal - institutional knowledge, help desk
	Searchable	

Security policy must be documented and available - often in a wiki model online
	Compliance - meeting laws, policies, regulations - industry specific or situational
		Penalties for not following, fines, firing, imprisonment
		Scope - domestic and international
	Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) - public company accounting reform and investor protection act 2002
	Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPPA) - healthcare standards for use of private health info
	Gramm-Leach-Biley Act of 1999 (GLBA) - disclosure of privacy info from finance orgs

Acceptable Use Policies (WUP)
	detailed docs on how you can use your companies tech
		covers internet use, phone use, mobile devices
		limits legal liability for the org

Password policies
	complex
	expire after 30, 60, 90 days or more often
	Account unlock process - should not be trivial

When someone leaves org, disable instead of delete

Inventory management - record of every tech asset, make and model, purchase date
	Devices tagged with barcodes, RFID, etc
